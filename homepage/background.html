About My Locations History


The location I choose is close to where I live. The train station on 116th street has faced massive change since the buildings on 114th street (Pictured to the left) got redone, and many of the old residents got pushed out. While this did help solve a major drug problem that had previously haunted the street for years, it brought in its own new issues. Additionally, many drug addicts moved into the aforementioned trainstation which they now use for shelter and a place to shoot up.
